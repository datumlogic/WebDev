Find Your Freedom

"It's not about the bands, its about the wristbands" - Ziv from London

Discover the festivals, enrich your experiences, and relive the memories.
Information, Mobile smart tech and social media; technology used by you in your daily lives is remasterd by fezzee. Freedom found! Long summer days and sultry nights, endless music and dancing all night. Partying with old friends and meeting new people. Watching your favorite bands, and seeing new ones. There's excitement in the air and it's electric, with lights dancing as the stars glisten. Drink in the mood, there are friendly people everywhere, laughing, relaxing. It’s all about the music, but more than just about the bands. Leave the stresses of life behind and spend a weekend in a field with 10’s of thousands of other hedonists like yourself. Forget your troubles and open yourself to whatever may come your way. Let the music set you free.

It's been said, that no so long ago, people danced at gigs, but now they click/share/tweet. While that may be a gross exhaggeration, your smart phone is arguably just as important an accessory at a festival as your toothbrush and your welly boots. It's your camera, your communication center, your social hub, and your map, at the least. It should let you roam, untethered. It should give you the information you need, when you need it, and should let you share your expereinces. It should help you be creative and be part of the creativity of others. It should help you conserve your battery and it should still be useful with little or no mobile connectivity, and when connected, it should rock.

Everywhere you are

Whether you are on the web, on your smart phone, or on social media sites, Fezzee will be there with you. Fezzee is a suite of apps, that let you plan your festival experieince, supports you at the sites, and lets you share you expereinces well after the gigs are over. Street Smart vs. Fezzee Smart.
